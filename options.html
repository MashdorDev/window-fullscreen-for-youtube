Window Fullscreen for YouTube

Settings sync across surfaces — the YouTube gear menu shows the most common toggles, everything is here.

Hotkey

Format: Shift+F, Ctrl+Alt+W, etc. Modifiers: Shift, Ctrl, Alt, Meta. Esc always exits.

Behavior

In scrollable mode, the page stays scrollable while the player is enlarged — you can scroll to see comments/related videos below.

When on, the chat panel stays fixed to the right edge and the comments below the video respect the chat width. When off, the chat sits in YouTube's normal column flow and scrolls with the page.

Hide while active

Granular control over what disappears when windowed fullscreen is on.
